Introduction of Nashik !

Nashik an ancient city and the largest city in the northern region of the Indian state of Maharashtra. Situated on the banks of river Godavari, Nashik is the fourth largest city in Maharashtra, after Mumbai, Pune and Nagpur. Nasik is well known for being one of the Hindu pilgrimage sites the kumbha Mela, which is held every 12 years.
